Cardiology Ultrasound

Cardiac ultrasound or echocardiography is a medical imaging exam that uses ultrasound to visualize the inside of the heart and all the structures that compose it, and to appreciate its functioning. Echocardiography is indicated as part of the diagnosis and monitoring of heart disease.
